About This Document
Purpose This record compiles the known executive officers of the University of Limerick Students’ Union and its successors (NIHE Limerick SU, ULSU, ULSL) from 1972 to present.
Sources Compiled from archived versions of ulsu.ie, ulwolves.ie, personal records, UL Student Life communications, and alumni input.
Notable Structural Changes In 2012/13, the executive structure was reduced from 5+ officers to 3. The name ULSU was replaced with UL Student Life (ULSL) in 2016/17.
Maintainer Eoghan O’Mahony (as of AY24/25)
